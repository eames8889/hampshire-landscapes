About Hampshire Landscapes

Based in the heart of Hampshire, we’re a locally owned business with over 15 years of experience in landscaping and garden design.

We’re passionate about creating outdoor spaces that are both beautiful and practical. Whether you’re after a stunning garden transformation or simple, low-maintenance upgrades, we bring expertise, attention to detail, and a friendly approach to every job.

Fully insured and committed to sustainability, we use eco-friendly materials wherever possible and always clean up after ourselves.
